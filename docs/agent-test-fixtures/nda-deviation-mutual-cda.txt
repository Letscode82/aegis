MUTUAL CONFIDENTIALITY AND NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered
into by and between Dr. Reddy's Laboratories Ltd., 8-2-337, Road No. 3,
Banjara Hills, Hyderabad 500034 ("DRL") and Vertex Diagnostics Pvt. Ltd.,
Chennai ("Counterparty").

PURPOSE: Evaluation of a potential co-development relationship.

1. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION. Each party may disclose proprietary technical,
   business and regulatory information to the other.

2. TERM OF CONFIDENTIALITY. The obligations of confidentiality under this
   Agreement shall be PERPETUAL and shall survive indefinitely, with no
   expiry, for all Confidential Information disclosed hereunder.

3. NON-SOLICITATION. Neither party shall solicit the employees of the other
   for a period of thirty-six (36) months.

4. GOVERNING LAW. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India and
   the parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts at
   Hyderabad.

5. RESIDUALS. The Receiving Party shall be free to use any information
   retained in the unaided memory of its personnel.

6. EXECUTION. This Agreement shall be signed the same day it is received.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF the parties have executed this Agreement.
