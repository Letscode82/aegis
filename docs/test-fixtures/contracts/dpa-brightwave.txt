DATA PROCESSING ADDENDUM

This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") forms part of the Master Services
Agreement between the Customer ("Controller") and Brightwave Analytics, Inc.
("Processor").

1. SCOPE. Processor processes personal data only on documented instructions
   from Controller and solely to provide the Services.

2. SUB-PROCESSORS. Processor may engage sub-processors provided it gives
   Controller thirty (30) days' prior notice of any addition or replacement
   and a right to object.

3. SECURITY. Processor implements appropriate technical and organisational
   measures appropriate to the risk, including encryption in transit and at
   rest.

4. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS. Where personal data of EU/UK data subjects is
   transferred outside the EEA/UK, the parties will rely on the Standard
   Contractual Clauses, incorporated by reference.

5. DATA-SUBJECT REQUESTS. Processor will assist Controller in responding to
   data-subject requests within the timelines required by applicable law.

6. AUDIT. Controller may audit Processor's compliance once per year on
   reasonable notice.

7. DELETION. On termination, Processor will delete or return all personal
   data at Controller's election.

8. BREACH NOTIFICATION. Processor will notify Controller without undue delay,
   and in any event within seventy-two (72) hours, of becoming aware of a
   personal-data breach.
